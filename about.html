Our Story

Stab Jakarta was founded with a passion for delicious food and a love for our community. We strive to provide the best burgers and rice bowls in Jakarta, made with fresh ingredients and cooked to perfection.

Our mission is to offer a unique dining experience that combines great taste with exceptional service. Whether you're dining in or ordering out, we aim to make every meal a memorable one.

Our Values

Quality

We use only the freshest ingredients to ensure the highest quality in every dish we serve.

Community

We believe in giving back to our community and creating a welcoming environment for everyone.

Sustainability

We are committed to sustainable practices and reducing our environmental impact.
